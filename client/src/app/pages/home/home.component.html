Introduction

The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign has one of the most diverse college student bodies in the world. However, while some majors have a wide array of demographics, other programs are more concentrated. This tool aims to provide information on the raw data, context for historical norms and racial norms, and progression of diversity within fields. Below, we have overall statistics of every college from publically available data (see About for more information).

Hover over any part of the bar chart to show the more details. Use the menu in the top left corner to see data on different colleges within UIUC. Within different colleges, click on the bar chart to learn more about the major!
